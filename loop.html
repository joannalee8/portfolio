Loop

Loop is a platform where companies are able to hold internal networking sessions remotely to encourage community building during the times where peers are unavailable to meet online.

ROLE

UX Researcher, UI Designer, Interviewer

TOOLS

Figma, Zoom, After Effects

TEAM

David Baik, Jonathan Choi

DURATION

4 Weeks

Loop is a platform that focuses on creating a sense of community for people working remotely as part of building a healthy work culture. By intervening at the beginning stages of building relationships, Loops creates an environment for people to meet others and form connections.
